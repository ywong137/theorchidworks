Orchid Care

Aloha! Like flowers that bloom in unexpected places, every orchid has its unique care needs. Please see below for general care tips:

Oncidium Orchid Care

  • Light: Bright, indirect light. Near an east or west-facing window is ideal.
  • Water: Allow to dry slightly between waterings. Avoid letting roots sit in water.
  • Humidity: Prefers 40–60% humidity. Misting helps.
  • Temperature: 65–75°F (18–24°C) daytime; slightly cooler at night.
  • Fertilizer: Use a balanced orchid fertilizer twice a month.
